# zecompaign — Product Summary

## What We Built

**zecompaign** is a **B2B SaaS collaboration platform** that helps marketing teams and agencies securely manage, share, and coordinate email campaigns across multiple SMTP accounts.

---

## The Core Positioning

### We Are:
- ✅ A **collaboration platform** for marketing teams
- ✅ A **secure credential vault** for SMTP accounts
- ✅ A **team workflow tool** with audit trails and permissions
- ✅ A **unified dashboard** for multi-account campaign management

### We Are NOT:
- ❌ An email service provider (like Mailchimp or SendGrid)
- ❌ A sending infrastructure (we use YOUR SMTP accounts)
- ❌ A marketing automation platform (like HubSpot)
- ❌ A CRM or contact manager

---

## The Problem We Solve (B2B)

**Before zecompaign:**
- 🔓 Teams share SMTP passwords via Slack → security risk
- 👥 No visibility into who sent what → chaos and duplicate work
- 🔀 Logging into 5+ different email platforms daily → wasted time
- 🚫 Can't grant partial access → all-or-nothing credentials
- 📊 No unified analytics → fragmented campaign data

**After zecompaign:**
- 🔐 All SMTP credentials in vault-encrypted storage
- 👥 Full team visibility with audit logs
- 🎯 One dashboard for all accounts
- ✅ Role-based access control per account
- 📊 Unified analytics across all campaigns

**Business Impact:** Save 10-15 hours/week, eliminate security risks, pass SOC 2 audits
